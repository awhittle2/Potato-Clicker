Sweet Corn and Potato Gratin


1 medium onion, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

2 pounds medium Yukon Gold potatoes

2 cups fresh or frozen corn

1 can (8-1/4 ounces) cream-style corn

3/4 cup panko bread crumbs

1 tablespoon butter, melted


  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Stir in flour, garlic, salt and pepper until blended; gradually add milk. Stir in potatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cook and stir until potatoes are crisp-tender, 8-10 minutes.
  2. Stir in corn and cream-style corn. Transfer to an 8-in. square baking dish coated with cooking spray.
  3. In a small bowl, combine bread crumbs and butter; sprinkle over potatoes. Bake until golden brown and potatoes are tender, 45-50 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
