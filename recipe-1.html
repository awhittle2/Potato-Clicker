Sweet Corn and Potato Gratin

1 medium onion, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 pounds medium Yukon Gold potatoes
2 cups fresh or frozen corn
1 can (8-1/4 ounces) cream-style corn
3/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tablespoon butter, melted

Step 2


Stir in flour, garlic, salt and pepper until blended; gradually add milk.

Step 3


Stir in potatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cook and stir until potatoes are crisp-tender, 8-10 minutes.

Step 4


Stir in corn and cream-style corn. Transfer to an 8-in. square baking dish coated with cooking spray.

Step 5


In a small bowl, combine bread crumbs and butter; sprinkle over potatoes.

Step 6


Bake until golden brown and potatoes are tender, 45-50 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
