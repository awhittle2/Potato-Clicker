Lemon and Garlic New Potatoes


1 pound small red potatoes

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 garlic cloves, minced

1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon pepper


  1. Cut scrubbed potatoes into wedges; place in a large saucepan. Add water to cover; bring to a boil.

  2. Cook, covered, until tender, 10-15 minutes; drain. In the same pan, heat oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Add potatoes; cook until browned, 4-6 minutes. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer.

  4. Remove from heat. Stir in remaining ingredients.
