Skillet Red Potatoes


2 tablespoons canola oil

2 medium red potatoes, cooked and cut into 1/2-inch chunks

1/2 teaspoon dried parsley flakes

1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon paprika


  1. In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat.

  2. Add potatoes; cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Stir in remaining ingredients.

  4. Cook and stir until potatoes are browned and tender, about 5 minutes longer..
