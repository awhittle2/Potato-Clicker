Air-Fryer Potato Chips


2 large potatoes

Olive oil-flavored cooking spray

1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Minced fresh parsley, optional


Procedures

Step 1


Preheat air fryer to 360Â°. Using a mandoline or vegetable peeler, cut potatoes into very thin slices.

Step 2


Transfer to a large bowl; add enough ice water to cover. Soak for 15 minutes; drain. Add more ice water and soak another 15 minutes.

Step 3


Drain potatoes; place on towels and pat dry. Spritz potatoes with cooking spray; sprinkle with salt.

Step 4


In batches, place potato slices in a single layer on greased tray in air-fryer basket.

Step 5


Cook until crisp and golden brown, 15-17 minutes, stirring and turning every 5-7 minutes. If desired, sprinkle with parsley.
